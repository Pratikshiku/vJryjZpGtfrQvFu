## Hospital Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/6a3e531a29f845dea010ed7137f5a82e/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): doctors, nurses, administrators.

**Doctor Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Internal Forum
5. Hospital Announcements
6. Personal Center
7. My Posts
8. Change Password
9. Personal Information
10. Bed Management
11. Pharmacy Management
12. Patient Information Management
13. Prescription Information Management

**Nurse Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Internal Forum
5. Hospital Announcements
6. Personal Center
7. My Posts
8. Change Password
9. Personal Information
10. Bed Information
11. Pharmacy Information
12. Patient Information
13. Prescription Information



**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Doctor Management
4. Nurse Management
5. Bed Category Management
6. Drug Category Management
7. Department Information Management
8. Internal Forum Management
9. Carousel Image Management
10. Hospital Announcements
